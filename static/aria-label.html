Demo aria-label

Without aria-label:

        
            <a href="https://twitter.com/WCEurope" class="fa fa-twitter"></a>

With aria-label:

        
            <a href="https://twitter.com/WCEurope" aria-label="WCEU on Twitter" class="fa fa-twitter"></a>

But beware: aria-label overrides link text:
WCEU on Facebook 

        
            <a href="https://www.facebook.com/WordCampEurope" aria-label="WCEU on Twitter" class="fa fa-facebook">
              WCEU on Facebook
            </a>
